About Me

Hello there! I'm a mobile-end developer and aspiring android developer based in Delhi, India.

Having consumed a large chunk of my life studying, I've finally found my passion at Technologies' crossroads. I started as a management trainee in a small startup, then worked some on websites and later went back to college to complete my computer science degree. After I rejoined my position as a mobile-end developer and continued my journey towards developing android based mobile applications, one lesson that survived during this journey was the quality of the end-user. Whether a real or digital product, its scope needs to be visible, and the result itself must be straightforward and easy to use. So no matter what I'm working on, I continuously have the user in mind and attempt my most excellent to create a pleasant experience.

When I'm free, you can find me obsessing over food, songs, and PlayStation, or working on one of my many hobbies like traveling. I tend to switch things up every few months, so if you want to know what I'm up to, follow me! :)
